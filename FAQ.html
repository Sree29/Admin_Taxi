Basic Questions
1. How do I book a taxi?

To book a taxi, simply go to our booking page, enter your pick-up and drop-off locations, select your preferred vehicle type, and choose a time slot. After providing your details, confirm the booking and you will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.

2. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking through your account dashboard. Please note that cancellations made less than 1 hour before the scheduled pick-up time may incur a cancellation fee.

3. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via the contact form on our website, or by calling our helpline.
